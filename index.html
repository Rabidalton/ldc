Welcome to the Liberia Diplomatic Club

A network and platform for youth development, interactive dialogue, and transformation through the practice and study of diplomacy.

Our Mission

we create a dynamic platform for youth development through the practice and study of diplomacy. We are committed to advancing global peace, justic, security, development, health and social inequalities.

By simulating international organizations like United Nations, European Union, and African Union - the Liberia Diplomatic Club provides aspiring leaders with hands-on training in diplomacy, leadership, public speaking, and negotiations skills

Our leadership conferences connect young professionals with relevant sectors actors to drive meaningful change.

Upcoming Events

March 2025 Conference - Sinkor Palace

Conference

DAY ONE features a diplomatic symposium with dignitaries, business leaders, students, and young professionals discussing key national and global issues, creating resolutions, and engaging in debates.

DAY TWO offers a tour of the Liberia National Museum, exploring the nation's history and role in diplomacy, providing hands-on learning to enhance leadership skills in diplomacy.

November 2025 Conference - EJS Ministerial complex

Conference

DAY ONE features a diplomatic symposium with dignitaries, business leaders, students, and young professionals discussing key national and global issues, creating resolutions, and engaging in debates.

DAY TWO offers a tour of the Libassa Ecolodge, to enjoy Liberia's wild and beautiful nature in an ecofriendly way.

July 2025 Conference - University of Liberia, Fendel Campus

symposium

DAY ONE features a diplomatic symposium with dignitaries, business leaders, students, and young professionals discussing key national and global issues, creating resolutions, and engaging in debates.

DAY TWO offers a visit at the U.S Embassy Library in Monrovia, exploring the US and Liberia sister relationship exchange and role in diplomacy, providing hands-on learning to enhance leadership skills in diplomacy.
